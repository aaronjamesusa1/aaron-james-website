About This Book:

Are You Tired of Chasing More—But Feeling Empty? In a world that screams "Buy now! Upgrade! You deserve it!", true freedom comes from a radical counterculture lifestyle—one that values generosity over accumulation and eternal treasures over temporary comforts.

In G.R.E.E.D, author and devoted follower of Christ Aaron A. James delivers a hard-hitting yet hope-filled roadmap to:

  • Break free from the soul-sucking cycle of materialism
  • Discover the joy of giving sacrificially—even when it hurts
  • Leave a legacy that outlives your bank account
  • Fund the Gospel with every dollar you release

Why This Book Is Different:

  • Raw & Relatable – No preachy platitudes, just real stories of struggle and victory
  • Biblically Grounded – Every chapter rooted in Scripture, not just self-help
  • Action-Packed – 90-day challenges, worksheets, and practical steps
  • Funds Ministry – 50% of proceeds go to feeding the hungry and supporting local churches
